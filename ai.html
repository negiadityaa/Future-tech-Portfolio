What is AI?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. Specific applications of AI include expert systems, natural language processing, speech recognition and machine vision.


In the coming years, AI will revolutionize every industry, from healthcare diagnostics to autonomous transportation. The aesthetics of code and the logic behind neural networks are converging to build an entirely new digital infrastructure.
