The Era of Automation

Robotics involves the design, construction, operation, and use of robots. As technology advances, these machines are stepping out of factories and into our daily lives, assisting in surgeries, exploring deep space, and managing complex logistics.


Combining mechanical engineering, electronic engineering, and computer science, modern robotics is creating intelligent machines that can adapt to their environment and perform tasks that are dangerous or impossible for humans.
